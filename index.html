Do you feel like you’re forever running on the treadmill and dieting and doing all the “right” steps, but never getting to finally have your ice cream?(the ice cream is your dream bod). Well, with determination, the right mindset and some serious bacon—whoops I mean elbow—greese, that ice cream can finally be yours. A lot of people underestimate themselves. They don’t think they are capable of achieving certain dreams (like a rockin’ bod) so they say it’ll happen when pigs fly, since pigs are way to out of shape to really move. Well, I’ve figured out some creative ways to enable pigs to pick up some new skills!

This site is your window to a new you. The best way to use it is to start with the motivation page. This will get you excited about getting into a new diet/exercise routine. Then go to BMI calculator to see where you are holding. You can also use the BMR calculator to see how many calories you natrually burn without exercise. This will help you gauge how much exercise you should do everyday.

And for those trying to lose weight (aren't we all?), we have a helpful program to suggest the number of calories to consume per day in order to reach your goal weight. And lastly, check out all the awesome exercises to get into rockin' shape!
