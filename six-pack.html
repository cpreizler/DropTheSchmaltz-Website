Try to do as many of these exercises as you can each day (just not on shabbos).

Sit-ups

3 sets of 20 reps--Or until you're sweating like a proper piggie
  1. Keep your feet under a sturdy item (or have someone stand on them) and lay all the way back with your knees bent
  2. Place your hands on opposite shoulders
  3. Without moving your neck, sit all the way up as if you are trying to take a bite out of a piece of cake resting on your knees
  4. SLOWLY move your back down to the ground
  5. Repeat from step 3

Leg Lifts

3 sets of 10 reps--Unless your piggie legs couldn't keep going up
  1. Lay flat on your back with your palms against the floor and your legs together
  2. Raise your feet towards shamayim until your legs are pointed straight up
  3. Make sure your hands and head are placed firmly on the floor
  4. SLOWLY bring your feet down until your heels touch the floor
  5. Repeat from step 2

Plank

For one minute--Or until you realize you've collapsed into the mud like a piggie
  1. Get into a push-up formation
  2. Rest on your forearms instead of your hands
  3. Make sure your body is flat
  4. Count to sixty!

Bicycle Crunches

2 cycles of 30 seconds--Or if your piggie tummy feels like it's in geheinom
  1. Lay flat on your back
  2. Lift your legs and move them as if you are pedaling on a bicycle
  3. Count to 30 as you make the pedaling motion
